Playback controls

With these controls you can play/stop a song or seek to the wished position.

Table 4.1. AGS playback controls table

iconactiondescription
backwardSeeking back until it's pressed again.
previousSeeking back until it's not pressed anymore.
playStarts the engines and the piano roll.
stopStops the engines and the piano roll.
nextSeeking forward until it's not pressed anymore.
forwardSeeking forward until it's pressed again.
